Komatsu and Pronto Launch Smart Quarry Autonomous for Quarry-Size Trucks

Available through Komatsu's dealer network, the system enables operators to retrofit existing fleets or specify autonomy on new equipment.

Komatsu North America and Pronto have jointly announced Komatsu Smart Quarry Autonomous, powered by Pronto. The system is available through Komatsu's dealer network across North America, giving operators the choice to retrofit autonomy onto existing fleet assets or specify it at the point of new truck purchase.

Smart Quarry Autonomous integrates Pronto's AHS software and sensor suite directly with Komatsu's Smart Quarry platform — enabling 24/7 autonomous operation, consistent haul cycle times, measurable fuel efficiency improvements, and real-time operational data insights. The integration gives quarry managers a single interface for both machine telemetry and autonomous dispatch management.

"Smart Quarry Autonomous is the answer — Pronto's proven AHS baked directly into the ecosystem quarry managers already use."

The product follows a 2023 pilot at Heidelberg Materials' Lake Bridgeport operation in Texas, where Komatsu HD605 and Caterpillar 775G trucks ran in mixed-OEM autonomous formation under Pronto AHS. Both OEM-agnostic operation across manufacturers and direct dealer channel access represent industry firsts for quarry-scale autonomous haulage — making it the most commercially accessible autonomous haulage product for sub-150-tonne operations to date.
